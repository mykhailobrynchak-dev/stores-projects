Bolt polygon coverage by VARUS stores, %

Coverage methodology. For each city, a union of 6 km circles (straight-line / as the crow flies) is built around the active VARUS stores. Coverage = the area of the Bolt delivery polygon that falls inside this zone, divided by the polygon's total area. Geometry is projected into a local metric system around the zone centroid. The combined Bucha+Irpin+Brovary+Vyshhorod polygon is split into separate zones and assigned to the nearest VARUS city.

Data. Active stores — dim_provider_v2 (group_name='VARUS', provider_status='active'). GMV — fact_order_delivery, order_state='delivered', order_created_date between 2026-04-01 and 2026-06-30, country_code='ua'. The % is calculated against the total VARUS GMV for the period. Bolt polygons — KML files from Bolt polygons.
